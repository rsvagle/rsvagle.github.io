VirtualScope

VirtualScope is a group project I lead that was conceived by a biology professor at Metropolitan State University needing to give her students access to their experiments remotely. The vision was to use a Raspberry Pi for streaming video online while capturing images from a microscope live for students who arenâ€™t on campus. A Raspberry Pi runs Python code to query a database, record and stream video, take photos, and open an ftp connection for upload to the cloud. I learned enough PHP, Python, and Bootstrap along the way to present dynamic web pages and define multiple levels of authorization to particular features. After the semester, Professor Harley was given additional grant money to further pursue the project based on our progress.
