Metro Data Collector

Metro Data Collector is a group project in which we created an Android app that allowed a user to record or import data in XML or JSON format for a scientific study. I took the leadership position during the final iteration, migrating our Java project to the Android platform. I also took the lead in applying and implementing the design patterns we learned in class such as the factory pattern, the singleton pattern, and the strategy pattern. The professor noted that my redesign and improved implementations allowed our group to massively improve our project for the final iteration, earning us a perfect grade.
